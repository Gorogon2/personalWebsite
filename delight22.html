Delight22.com Digital Marketing Project

Technologies Used: Wordpress | HTML5/CSS | Pinterest | Paid/Organic Ad Campaigns | Tailwinds | Graphic Design

In early 2019, shortly after receiving my Google Adwords Certification, I created a brand called Delight22. I designed a Wordpress website, where I created content and landing pages for my Pinterest campaigns to funnel to. I designed ads using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and then ran both paid and organic ads. Through monitoring and analytics, I was able to adjust my campaigns with regularity, which gave my site large amounts of traffic and turned into conversions.
This was my first experience with Pinterest and I learned a lot about social media marketing by using the platform. I was able to create several organic ads that went viral and resulted in 10,000s of impressions within just a few days. Within 1 month I went from no impressions to over 140k impressions.
